The Risks of Smoking

Smoking is a dangerous habit that poses serious risks to your health. It can lead to a variety of health problems, including:

  • Lung cancer
  • Heart disease
  • Respiratory infections
  • Stroke
  • Chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD)

Here is a table illustrating some of the statistics related to smoking:

Statistic Smokers Non-Smokers
Death rate from lung cancer 20% 1%
Heart disease risk 2x higher Lower
COPD risk 10x higher Lower
